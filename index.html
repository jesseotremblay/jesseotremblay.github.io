About Me

Hi, my name is Jesse. I'm a 33 year old former small business owner and marketing consultant. I've been designing graphics and web pages as a hobby since the Dawson's Creek cast were still in high school. After a long journey through manufacturing, professional soccer coaching, business ownership and eventually consulting, I've decided to pursue my real career passion, web design. Thanks for checking out my page!

Over the past few years I have been immersing myself in learning how to create digital experiences for users. Aside from my freelance work, I have also attended the highly selective Startup Institute where I honed my skills in web design and user experience design.
